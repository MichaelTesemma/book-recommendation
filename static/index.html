What Do These Dimensions Mean?

⏱️

Pacing

How fast the story moves. Low = slow-burn, meditative. High = page-turner, relentless.

🧠

Character Depth

How complex and layered the characters are. Low = archetypal, simple. High = psychologically rich, contradictory.

🔀

Plot Complexity

How intricate the narrative structure is. Low = straightforward. High = multi-threaded, layered reveals.

✍️

Prose Quality

The literary craftsmanship of the writing. Low = functional, accessible. High = lyrical, beautifully crafted.

💭

Philosophical Depth

How thought-provoking the themes are. Low = entertaining, light. High = existential, challenges assumptions.

❤️‍🔥

Emotional Intensity

How emotionally powerful and moving. Low = detached, cool. High = gut-wrenching, cathartic.

😂

Humor

How comedic and funny the book is. Low = serious, dramatic. High = witty, laugh-out-loud.

💥

Action

How dynamic and event-driven. Low = introspective, calm. High = explosive, physical.
